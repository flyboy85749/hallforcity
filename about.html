About Caryn

Caryn is originally from Hancock, New York, and graduated from Hancock Central School in 1984. After high school, Caryn attended Central Cities Business Institute in Syracuse, where she earned a B.S. in Accounting.

She then attended Bethany Divinity School in Dothan, Alabama.

Caryn came to Florida on sportfish from Montauk, Long Island at 21 years of age, as live aboard First Mate in Florida and Mexico

Caryn spent her 20’s on Jupiter Island during the season, and traveling in the off-season

She has also lived with tribes in Bolivia and Papua New Guinea, and also visited New Zealand and Nigeria

Caryn was married at 29, but continued to be employed at Jupiter Island in Gil Cavanaugh’s Golf Shop and nannied for the Adrian (Bear) Reed family

Caryn developed a program for inmates titled “It Ain’t Over” and hopes that it comes to fruition in 2020

Caryn plays many instruments and can speak Spanish and Hebrew (mostly blessings)
